The top 25 scorers in National Football League history are all placekickers. Statistics include regular season scoring only.\n

Active players are listed in bold (age in parentheses).\n

Statistics are accurate as of end of the 2020 season.[1]\n

Non-kickers[edit]

\n

The top five scoring non-kickers in NFL history are listed here with their overall scoring rank. Only one non-kicker, Jerry Rice, is in the top 50 scorers of all-time (although George Blanda played as both a quarterback and a kicker, 1,966 of his 2,002 points were scored as a kicker).\n
